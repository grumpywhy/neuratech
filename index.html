What Can MDM Token Do?

Our Solana-powered token creates a healthcare ecosystem that rewards participation, ensures privacy, and improves access to quality care.

MDM Token Utility

1 Payments & Discounts

Pay for telehealth consultations, premium AI diagnosis features, and subscription services using MDM tokens at discounted rates compared to traditional payment methods. Users who pay with MDM receive priority appointment scheduling and reduced wait times.

2 Data Contribution Rewards

Earn MDM tokens by contributing anonymized health data to research initiatives. Set permissions for how your data is used and automatically receive compensation when it helps advance medical research. The more comprehensive and consistent your data, the higher the rewards.

3 Health Achievement Incentives

Complete health challenges like maintaining medication adherence, hitting fitness goals, or attending preventative screenings to earn MDM tokens. These incentives promote better health outcomes while rewarding patients for taking control of their wellness journey.

4 Provider Verification & Ranking

Healthcare providers stake MDM tokens to join our network, with higher stakes creating greater visibility. Patients rate providers after consultations, affecting the provider's reputation score. Top-rated providers receive additional token rewards from the ecosystem fund.

5 Governance & Proposals

MDM token holders can vote on platform upgrades, research funding allocations, and new features proportional to their holdings. Submit improvement proposals and receive tokens if your ideas are implemented. This creates a community-driven healthcare platform that evolves based on user needs.

6 Tokenized Health Record Access

Generate time-limited access NFTs for your health records, controlling exactly what information providers can see and for how long. Revoke access at any time, creating an immutable audit trail of who accessed your data. Providers pay small MDM fees to request access, which go directly to the patient.
